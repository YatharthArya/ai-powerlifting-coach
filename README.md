# AI Powerlifting Coach

> **Better Decisions. Better Performance. Closer to Human Potential.**

---

# Project Overview

AI Powerlifting Coach is the first implementation of an **Evidence-Based Human Performance Decision Ecosystem**.

The ecosystem is designed to improve the quality, transparency, traceability, and consistency of human performance decisions through validated evidence rather than assumptions or guesswork.

Powerlifting serves as the first domain for validating the ecosystem before expanding into broader human performance applications.

---

# Vision

To build an ecosystem that continuously improves human understanding and decision-making by aligning them as closely as possible with reality through validated evidence.

The long-term vision extends beyond software to support athletes, coaches, strength and conditioning professionals, sports scientists, physiotherapists, researchers, and performance organizations.

---

# Mission

Transform human performance decision-making from intuition-driven practice into a transparent, explainable, evidence-based, and continuously improving process.

---

# Core Principles

The ecosystem is built upon several fundamental principles.

- Reality exists independently of observation.
- Observations approximate reality.
- Context gives observations meaning.
- Evidence precedes decisions.
- Human judgment remains the final authority.
- Every decision remains explainable and traceable.
- Knowledge continuously evolves through validated evidence.
