# Research

This directory contains active architectural research that has not yet matured into formal discoveries or permanent architecture.

Research documents are intentionally exploratory and may change frequently as new observations, challenges, and evidence emerge.

Unlike architectural documents, research notes do not represent validated principles and should not be treated as authoritative.

Research typically progresses through the following lifecycle:

Observation

↓

Hypothesis

↓

Working Notes

↓

Architectural Challenge

↓

Discovery

↓

Architecture

↓

Constitution (if fundamental)
